Fluffy American Pancakes

Light, airy, and golden brown pancakes perfect for a satisfying breakfast. These classic American-style pancakes pair beautifully with maple syrup and butter.

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 15 mins
Serves: 4
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter (plus more for greasing)
  • Maple syrup and butter for serving

Instructions

  1. 1

    Mix dry ingredients

    In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.

  2. 2

    Combine wet ingredients

    In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, and melted butter.

  3. 3

    Make the batter

    Pour the wet mixture into the dry ingredients and stir just until combined. Do not overmix; lumps are okay.

  4. 4

    Cook pancakes

    Heat a lightly greased non-stick skillet over medium heat. Pour 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.

  5. 5

    Serve

    Serve hot with a pat of butter and generous drizzle of maple syrup.

  • Add chocolate chips or blueberries to the batter for variety.
