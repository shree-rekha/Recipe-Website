Poha

A light and flavorful Indian breakfast made with flattened rice, onions, spices, and crunchy peanuts. Quick, healthy, and satisfying.

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 10 mins
Serves: 2
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flattened rice (poha)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1/4 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/4 cup roasted peanuts
  • 6‚Äì8 curry leaves
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander & lemon juice for garnish

Instructions

  1. 1

    Rinse Poha

    Rinse the flattened rice under water until soft. Drain completely and keep aside.

  2. 2

    Temper Spices

    In a pan, heat oil. Add mustard seeds, curry leaves, and green chilies. Let them splutter.

  3. 3

    Saut√© Onions

    Add chopped onions and cook until soft and translucent.

  4. 4

    Add Poha

    Add turmeric, salt, and soaked poha. Mix gently until evenly colored and heated through.

  5. 5

    Finish & Serve

    Add peanuts, mix well. Garnish with coriander leaves and a squeeze of lemon. Serve hot.

Chef's Tips

  • Use thick poha for best texture; avoid over-soaking.
  • Add boiled peas or grated carrots for more nutrition.
  • For extra crunch, add sev on top while serving.
  • Serve with tea or curd for a complete breakfast.
