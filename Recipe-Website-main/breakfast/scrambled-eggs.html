Scrambled Eggs with Bacon

Soft, fluffy scrambled eggs paired with crispy American bacon. A comforting and protein-rich breakfast that‚Äôs quick and easy to make.

Prep: 5 mins
Cook: 10 mins
Serves: 2
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 4 strips of bacon

Instructions

  1. 1

    Cook the bacon

    Place bacon strips in a skillet over medium heat. Cook until crispy, then remove and set aside on paper towels.

  2. 2

    Whisk the eggs

    In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper until well combined.

  3. 3

    Scramble the eggs

    In a clean non-stick skillet, melt the butter over low heat. Pour in the egg mixture and stir gently with a spatula until curds form and eggs are just set.

  4. 4

    Serve

    Serve scrambled eggs immediately with crispy bacon on the side. Enjoy with toast or hash browns if desired.

Chef's Tips

  • Use low heat for creamy scrambled eggs.
  • Add a dash of cream cheese or sour cream for extra richness.
  • Crumble cooked bacon into the eggs for a twist.
  • Serve with hot sauce for added kick.
