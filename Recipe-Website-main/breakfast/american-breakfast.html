Fluffy Pancakes

Classic American pancakes, golden and fluffy.

  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, eggs, milk, and butter.
  2. Heat a greased pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour batter and cook until bubbles form; flip and cook until golden.
  4. Serve with maple syrup and butter.

Scrambled Eggs & Bacon

Soft scrambled eggs with crispy American bacon.

  1. Whisk eggs with salt, pepper, and milk.
  2. Cook bacon strips in a skillet until crispy; set aside.
  3. Pour egg mixture into the pan and stir gently until soft and fluffy.
  4. Serve eggs with bacon on the side.

Hash Browns

Crispy shredded potatoes, fried to perfection.

  1. Peel and grate potatoes. Rinse and squeeze out excess water.
  2. Mix with salt and pepper.
  3. Press into a hot oiled pan and cook until golden and crispy on both sides.
  4. Serve hot with ketchup or eggs.
