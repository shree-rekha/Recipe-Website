Aloo Paratha

Stuffed Indian flatbread with spiced mashed potatoes.

  1. Prepare dough with wheat flour, water, and salt.
  2. Make filling using boiled potatoes, onions, and spices.
  3. Roll dough, stuff with filling, and roll again.
  4. Cook on tawa with ghee until golden brown.

Poha

Flattened rice dish with onions, spices, and peanuts.

  1. Rinse poha and let it soften.
  2. Heat oil, mustard seeds, curry leaves, and onions.
  3. Add turmeric, green chilies, and softened poha.
  4. Top with peanuts, coriander, and lemon juice.
