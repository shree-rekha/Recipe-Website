Crispy Hash Browns

Crispy on the outside and tender inside, these homemade hash browns are a breakfast classic and pair perfectly with eggs, bacon, or toast.

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 10 mins
Serves: 2
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 large russet potatoes, peeled
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: 1 tablespoon butter for extra flavor

Instructions

  1. 1

    Shred the potatoes

    Use a grater to shred the peeled potatoes. Place them in a bowl of cold water and soak for 5 minutes to remove excess starch.

  2. 2

    Drain and dry

    Drain the shredded potatoes and squeeze out as much water as possible using a clean kitchen towel or paper towels.

  3. 3

    Heat oil

    In a skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add butter if using for flavor.

  4. 4

    Cook the hash browns

    Add the shredded potatoes in a thin, even layer. Cook without stirring for 4‚Äì5 minutes until golden brown on the bottom.

  5. 5

    Flip and finish

    Flip the hash browns and cook the other side until golden and crisp, about 4‚Äì5 minutes more. Season with salt and pepper and serve hot.

Chef's Tips

  • Make sure the potatoes are well-drained to avoid soggy hash browns.
  • Add finely chopped onions for extra flavor.
  • Use a cast iron skillet for maximum crispiness.
  • Top with cheese or a fried egg for a hearty twist.
