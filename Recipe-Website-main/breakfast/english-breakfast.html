Full English Breakfast

Traditional British breakfast with eggs, sausage, bacon, and more.

  1. Fry sausages, bacon, and black pudding until browned.
  2. Grill tomatoes and mushrooms.
  3. Fry eggs sunny side up.
  4. Serve with baked beans and buttered toast.

Beans on Toast

Simple, hearty dish of baked beans over buttered toast.

  1. Heat baked beans in a saucepan.
  2. Toast slices of bread and butter them.
  3. Pour hot beans over toast.
  4. Optional: sprinkle cheese or herbs on top.

English Porridge

Warm and creamy oats perfect for chilly mornings.

  1. Combine oats and milk in a saucepan.
  2. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently.
  3. Simmer until thick and creamy.
  4. Top with honey, fruits, or nuts.
