Eggs Benedict

Classic eggs Benedict with hollandaise sauce.

  1. Prepare hollandaise sauce.
  2. Poach eggs until whites are set and yolks are runny.
  3. Toast English muffins.
  4. Assemble with ham, poached eggs, and hollandaise sauce.

Fruit Salad

Colorful fruit salad with a variety of fresh fruits.

  1. Wash and chop assorted fruits (strawberries, kiwi, mango, etc.).
  2. Combine in a large bowl.
  3. Add a squeeze of fresh lemon juice.
  4. Chill in the refrigerator before serving.
