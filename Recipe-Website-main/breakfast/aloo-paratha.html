Aloo Paratha

A traditional North Indian stuffed flatbread filled with spicy mashed potatoes. Crispy, flavorful, and perfect with butter, pickle, or curd.

Prep: 25 mins
Cook: 15 mins
Serves: 4
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • Water (as needed to knead dough)
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 boiled potatoes
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • Ghee or oil for roasting

Instructions

  1. 1

    Prepare the Dough

    Combine wheat flour, salt, and water. Knead into a soft dough and let it rest for 15‚Äì20 minutes.

  2. 2

    Make the Filling

    Mash boiled potatoes. Add onion, green chilies, spices, salt, and coriander. Mix well.

  3. 3

    Stuff the Paratha

    Roll a ball of dough into a small circle. Place the filling in the center, seal edges, and roll again into a flat round paratha.

  4. 4

    Cook the Paratha

    Heat a tawa (griddle). Cook each side with ghee or oil until golden brown and crispy.

  5. 5

    Serve Hot

    Serve with butter, yogurt, or pickle.

Chef's Tips

  • Use mashed potatoes without lumps to avoid tearing the dough.
  • Adjust spice levels based on your taste.
  • Serve hot for best flavor and texture.
  • You can add grated cheese for a twist.
