Full English Breakfast

A traditional British breakfast loaded with eggs, sausage, bacon, beans, toast, and more. It's hearty, satisfying, and perfect for weekends or special occasions.

Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 20 mins
Serves: 2
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 pork sausages
  • 2 slices of back bacon
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 slices of black pudding (optional)
  • 4 mushrooms
  • 1 tomato, halved
  • 1 cup baked beans
  • 2 slices of bread
  • Butter for toast
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. 1

    Fry the meats

    In a large skillet, fry sausages, bacon, and black pudding over medium heat until browned and cooked through.

  2. 2

    Grill veggies

    Grill or pan-fry the tomato and mushrooms until tender and slightly charred.

  3. 3

    Fry the eggs

    In a non-stick pan, fry eggs sunny-side-up or to your liking. Season with salt and pepper.

  4. 4

    Heat the beans

    Warm the baked beans in a saucepan over low heat, stirring occasionally.

  5. 5

    Toast and serve

    Toast the bread and butter it. Arrange all components on a plate and serve hot.

Chef's Tips

  • Use a grill or broiler for crispier bacon.
  • For a healthier version, bake instead of fry ingredients.
  • Add hash browns or fried bread for extra authenticity.
  • Black pudding is traditional but optional.
