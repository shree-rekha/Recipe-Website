Beans on Toast

A classic and comforting British breakfast dish. This easy recipe is perfect for a quick, filling, and delicious meal that anyone can prepare in minutes.

Prep: 2 mins
Cook: 5 mins
Serves: 2
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 can of baked beans (400g)
  • 4 slices of bread
  • Butter for spreading
  • Optional: grated cheese, herbs, or chili flakes

Instructions

  1. 1

    Heat the beans

    Pour baked beans into a small saucepan and heat gently over medium heat, stirring occasionally.

  2. 2

    Toast the bread

    Toast the bread slices until golden and crisp, then butter them while hot.

  3. 3

    Assemble

    Place the toast on plates and spoon the hot beans evenly over the slices.

  4. 4

    Add toppings

    Top with grated cheese, fresh herbs, or chili flakes if desired. Serve immediately.

Chef's Tips

  • Use whole grain or sourdough for added texture and nutrition.
  • For a vegan version, use dairy-free butter and check that the beans are vegan-friendly.
  • Add a fried or poached egg on top for extra protein.
  • Try spicy baked beans for a flavor kick!
