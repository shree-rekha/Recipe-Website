Classic Eggs Benedict

A timeless breakfast favorite featuring poached eggs, toasted English muffins, savory ham, and creamy hollandaise sauce.

Prep: 20 mins
Cook: 15 mins
Serves: 2
Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 2 English muffins, split and toasted
  • 4 large eggs
  • 4 slices of Canadian bacon or ham
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar (for poaching)
  • Chopped chives or parsley for garnish
  • Hollandaise Sauce:
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (melted)
  • Pinch of salt and cayenne pepper

Instructions

  1. 1

    Make the Hollandaise Sauce

    Whisk egg yolks and lemon juice in a bowl over simmering water. Slowly whisk in melted butter until thickened. Season with salt and cayenne. Keep warm.

  2. 2

    Poach the Eggs

    Bring water and vinegar to a gentle simmer. Crack eggs into a cup and gently slide them into the water. Cook for 3‚Äì4 minutes. Remove with a slotted spoon.

  3. 3

    Cook the Bacon

    Heat a skillet over medium heat and cook bacon slices until heated through and lightly browned.

  4. 4

    Assemble

    Place bacon on each muffin half, top with a poached egg, and drizzle with warm hollandaise sauce. Garnish with chives.

Chef's Tips

  • Use fresh eggs for the best poached egg shape.
  • Keep hollandaise warm by placing it in a bowl over warm water.
  • Use a blender for quicker hollandaise sauce preparation.
  • Try replacing bacon with smoked salmon or spinach for variety.
