Sweet Corn Soup

A light and creamy Indo-Chinese style soup featuring sweet corn, vegetables, and mild spices‚Äîperfect for lunch or as a cozy appetizer.

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 15 mins
Serves: 3
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped carrots
  • 1/2 cup sweet corn (boiled or canned)
  • 1/4 cup chopped cabbage
  • 1 tbsp cornflour mixed with 2 tbsp water
  • 3 cups vegetable broth or water
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • Spring onions for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. 1

    Saut√© vegetables

    In a pan, heat oil and saut√© carrots and cabbage for 2‚Äì3 minutes until slightly soft.

  2. 2

    Add corn and broth

    Add sweet corn and pour in vegetable broth. Bring to a boil.

  3. 3

    Thicken soup

    Slowly add the cornflour slurry while stirring continuously. Simmer until the soup thickens slightly.

  4. 4

    Season and serve

    Add salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with spring onions if desired and serve hot.

Chef's Tips

  • You can blend a portion of the corn for a creamier texture.
  • Use chicken broth instead of vegetable broth for a non-veg version.
  • Add soy sauce or a dash of vinegar for a more Chinese-style twist.
  • For extra spice, top with a little red chili sauce.
