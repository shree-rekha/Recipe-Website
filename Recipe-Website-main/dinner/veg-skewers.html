Grilled Veggie Skewers

Colorful and healthy skewers packed with marinated vegetables, grilled to perfection. A delightful dish for any summer meal.

Prep: 20 mins
Cook: 15 mins
Serves: 4
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into squares
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, cut into squares
  • 1 red onion, cut into chunks
  • 8‚Äì10 mushrooms, halved
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp dried oregano or Italian herbs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden or metal skewers

Instructions

  1. 1

    Soak skewers

    If using wooden skewers, soak them in water for 30 minutes to prevent burning.

  2. 2

    Marinate vegetables

    In a bowl, toss all vegetables with olive oil, garlic powder, oregano, salt, and pepper.

  3. 3

    Assemble skewers

    Thread marinated vegetables onto skewers, alternating types for color and texture.

  4. 4

    Grill

    Grill skewers over medium heat, turning occasionally, for 10‚Äì15 minutes until vegetables are tender and lightly charred.

Chef's Tips

  • Add a sprinkle of feta cheese or balsamic glaze before serving for extra flavor.
  • Try adding cherry tomatoes, baby corn, or paneer for variety.
  • Use metal skewers for easier grilling and reusable convenience.
  • Pair with grilled meats or serve as a vegetarian main dish.
