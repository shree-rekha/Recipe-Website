Fish Tacos

Crispy fish, tangy slaw, and creamy sauce wrapped in warm tortillas‚Äîthese fish tacos are a coastal favorite!

Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 10 mins
Serves: 3‚Äì4
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 500g white fish fillets (like cod or tilapia)
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise or sour cream
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 6‚Äì8 small tortillas
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. 1

    Season and cook fish

    Season fish with chili powder, salt, and pepper. Cook in a pan with a little oil until golden and flaky. Set aside.

  2. 2

    Prepare sauce

    Mix mayo or sour cream with lime juice and a pinch of salt for a simple creamy sauce.

  3. 3

    Warm tortillas

    Warm tortillas on a skillet or in the microwave until soft and pliable.

  4. 4

    Assemble tacos

    Place pieces of fish in tortillas. Top with shredded cabbage and drizzle with sauce. Add optional toppings like avocado, salsa, or cilantro.

Chef's Tips

  • Use corn tortillas for a more authentic touch.
  • Beer-batter the fish for extra crunch.
  • Add pickled onions or jalape√±os for tang and heat.
  • Try using Greek yogurt in the sauce for a healthier option.
