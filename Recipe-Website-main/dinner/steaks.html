BBQ Chicken

Grilled chicken glazed with smoky barbecue sauce.

  1. Marinate chicken in BBQ sauce for 1 hour.
  2. Grill on medium heat until cooked through.
  3. Brush with extra sauce during final minutes.
  4. Serve with coleslaw and fries.

Grilled Veggie Skewers

Colorful skewers with marinated vegetables grilled to perfection.

  1. Cut zucchini, bell peppers, mushrooms, and onions.
  2. Marinate with olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and herbs.
  3. Thread onto skewers and grill until charred and tender.
  4. Serve with yogurt dip or hummus.
