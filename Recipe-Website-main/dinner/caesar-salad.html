Caesar Salad

A crisp and creamy romaine salad tossed with parmesan, croutons, and a tangy Caesar dressing ‚Äî a true classic.

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 0 mins
Serves: 2
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 romaine lettuce head, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1/2 cup croutons
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. 1

    Prepare Lettuce

    Wash and chop romaine lettuce. Dry thoroughly.

  2. 2

    Add Dressing

    Place lettuce in a bowl and toss with Caesar dressing until coated.

  3. 3

    Add Toppings

    Top with croutons, grated parmesan, and freshly ground pepper.

  4. 4

    Serve Immediately

    Serve cold as a side dish or add grilled chicken for a full meal.

Chef's Tips

  • Use freshly grated parmesan for best flavor.
  • Add grilled chicken, shrimp, or tofu for protein.
  • Homemade croutons give a better crunch ‚Äî toast cubed bread with olive oil and garlic.
  • Chill your salad bowl before serving for a refreshing touch.
