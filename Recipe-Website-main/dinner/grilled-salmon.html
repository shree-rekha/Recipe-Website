Grilled Salmon

Perfectly grilled salmon fillet seasoned with lemon and herbs. A light, flavorful, and healthy seafood dish that's ready in minutes.

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 10 mins
Serves: 2
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • Fresh herbs (dill or parsley) for garnish

Instructions

  1. 1

    Prepare the salmon

    Pat dry the salmon fillets. Rub with olive oil, lemon juice, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.

  2. 2

    Preheat the grill

    Heat grill or grill pan to medium-high. Brush with oil to prevent sticking.

  3. 3

    Grill the salmon

    Place salmon skin-side down on grill. Cook for 6‚Äì8 minutes. Flip and cook 2‚Äì3 more minutes.

  4. 4

    Rest and serve

    Remove from grill and let rest for 5 minutes. Garnish with herbs and serve with vegetables or rice.

  • Try adding a garlic herb butter on top for richness.
