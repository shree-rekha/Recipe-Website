Veg Hakka Noodles

A popular Indo-Chinese dish made with stir-fried vegetables and noodles in savory sauces‚Äîquick, delicious, and packed with flavor.

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 15 mins
Serves: 2
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 150g Hakka noodles
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup julienned carrots
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup chopped spring onions
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp chili sauce (optional)
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. 1

    Boil Noodles

    Cook noodles in boiling water as per package instructions. Drain and toss in a little oil to prevent sticking.

  2. 2

    Stir-fry Veggies

    Heat oil in a wok. Add cabbage, carrots, peppers, and spring onions. Stir-fry on high heat for 2‚Äì3 minutes.

  3. 3

    Add Sauces

    Add soy sauce, vinegar, chili sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix well.

  4. 4

    Toss Noodles

    Add the cooked noodles and toss everything together for 1‚Äì2 minutes until well coated.

  5. 5

    Serve

    Serve hot garnished with extra spring onions if desired.

Chef's Tips

  • Use high heat to get the classic stir-fry texture.
  • Pre-boiled and cooled noodles work best to avoid sogginess.
  • Customize with mushrooms, beansprouts, or tofu for extra protein.
  • Avoid overcooking veggies‚Äîthey should remain slightly crisp.
