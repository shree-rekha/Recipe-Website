BBQ Chicken

Tender grilled chicken glazed with rich, smoky barbecue sauce. A summer favorite that's packed with flavor.

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 25 mins
Serves: 4
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken thighs or drumsticks
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika (optional)

Instructions

  1. 1

    Marinate Chicken

    In a bowl, combine chicken with half of the BBQ sauce, olive oil, salt, pepper, and smoked paprika. Let it marinate for at least 1 hour.

  2. 2

    Preheat Grill

    Preheat your grill to medium heat. Lightly oil the grill grates to prevent sticking.

  3. 3

    Grill Chicken

    Place marinated chicken on the grill. Cook for 6‚Äì7 minutes on each side, brushing with more BBQ sauce as it cooks.

  4. 4

    Glaze & Finish

    During the final minutes, brush with remaining BBQ sauce and let it caramelize. Ensure internal temp reaches 165¬∞F (75¬∞C).

Chef's Tips

  • Use a meat thermometer to ensure chicken is fully cooked.
  • Reserve some BBQ sauce separately to avoid cross-contamination.
  • For extra smoky flavor, use a charcoal grill or add wood chips.
  • Serve with classic sides like coleslaw, potato salad, or corn on the cob.
