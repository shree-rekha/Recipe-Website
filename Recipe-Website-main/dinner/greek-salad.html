Greek Salad

A refreshing and vibrant salad made with crisp vegetables, olives, feta cheese, and a zesty dressing.

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 0 mins
Serves: 2
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup black olives
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. 1

    Prepare the veggies

    Chop the tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion. Place them in a salad bowl.

  2. 2

    Add the toppings

    Add black olives and crumbled feta cheese to the bowl.

  3. 3

    Make the dressing

    In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper.

  4. 4

    Toss and serve

    Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently. Serve chilled.

Chef's Tips

  • Use Kalamata olives for a more authentic flavor.
  • Let the salad chill for 15 minutes before serving to enhance taste.
  • Pair it with grilled chicken or pita bread for a complete meal.
  • Use cherry tomatoes for a sweeter bite.
