Creamy Alfredo Pasta

Indulgent and comforting pasta coated in a creamy garlic parmesan sauce. A timeless Italian-American classic that's easy and delicious.

Prep: 5 mins
Cook: 15 mins
Serves: 2
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 200g fettuccine or pasta of choice
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley (optional)

Instructions

  1. 1

    Boil Pasta

    Cook the pasta in salted water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water and drain.

  2. 2

    Prepare Sauce Base

  3. 3

    Add Cream and Cheese

    Pour in heavy cream. Stir and simmer for 2 minutes. Add parmesan and stir until smooth.

  4. 4

    Mix Pasta

    Add cooked pasta to the sauce. Toss to coat. Use reserved pasta water if needed for consistency.

  5. 5

    Serve

    Garnish with parsley and serve immediately.
