Tomato Basil Soup

This creamy tomato basil soup is rich, comforting, and made with fresh tomatoes and fragrant basil. Perfect for pairing with grilled cheese!

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 25 mins
Serves: 4
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 5-6 ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Chef's Tips

  • Use canned San Marzano tomatoes if fresh ones aren't available.
  • Add a pinch of sugar if the tomatoes are too acidic.
  • Top with croutons or a swirl of cream for a fancier touch.
  • Pairs excellently with a grilled cheese sandwich!
