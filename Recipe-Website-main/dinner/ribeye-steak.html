Grilled Ribeye Steak

A juicy, perfectly grilled ribeye steak topped with garlic butter. Ideal for dinner gatherings or a special night in.

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 10 mins
Serves: 2
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 ribeye steaks (1-inch thick)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. 1

    Season the Steaks

    Pat steaks dry and season both sides with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Rub with olive oil.

  2. 2

    Grill the Steaks

    Preheat grill to high heat. Grill steaks for 4‚Äì5 minutes per side, depending on thickness and preferred doneness.

  3. 3

    Make Garlic Butter

    While steaks grill, melt butter and stir in minced garlic. Set aside.

  4. 4

    Rest and Serve

    Let steaks rest for 5 minutes. Drizzle with garlic butter and garnish with parsley. Serve hot.

Chef's Tips

  • Let the steaks come to room temperature before grilling for even cooking.
  • Use a meat thermometer: 130¬∞F for medium-rare, 140¬∞F for medium.
  • Resting the steak allows juices to redistribute, keeping it tender.
  • Pair with mashed potatoes or grilled vegetables for a complete meal.
