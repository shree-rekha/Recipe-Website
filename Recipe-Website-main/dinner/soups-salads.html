Sweet Corn Soup

Delicate Indo-Chinese style soup with veggies.

  1. Boil corn, carrots, and beans.
  2. Add corn paste, thicken, season.
  3. Add pepper, soy sauce, garnish.
  4. Serve hot.

Greek Salad

Refreshing salad with tomato, olives, feta.

  1. Chop tomatoes, cucumbers, olives.
  2. Add feta, drizzle olive oil & vinegar.
  3. Season and toss gently.
  4. Serve chilled.

Caesar Salad

Classic romaine salad with Caesar dressing.

  1. Chop romaine lettuce and add croutons.
  2. Mix in parmesan and Caesar dressing.
  3. Top with grilled chicken (optional).
  4. Serve chilled.
