Iced Tea

Refreshing and cool iced tea, perfect for hot summer days.

Prep: 5 mins
Cook: 5 mins
Serves: 4
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • 4 tea bags (black tea or green tea)
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • Ice cubes
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. 1

    Boil Water

    In a saucepan, bring water to a boil.

  2. 2

    Steep Tea

    Remove from heat, add tea bags, and let steep for 5 minutes.

  3. 3

    Add Sugar

    Stir in sugar until dissolved. Let cool to room temperature.

  4. 4

    Serve Iced

    Fill glasses with ice cubes and lemon slices. Pour tea over ice and garnish with mint leaves.

Chef's Tips

  • Add honey or agave syrup as a natural sweetener.
  • For a stronger tea flavor, steep tea bags longer.
  • Experiment with different tea varieties for unique flavors.
  • Use fresh lemon juice for a tangier taste.
