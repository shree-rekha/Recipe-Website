Chocolate Chip Cookies

Classic soft and chewy cookies loaded with melty chocolate chips. Perfect with a glass of milk or for any sweet craving!

Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 12 mins
Makes: 24 cookies
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (225g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. 1

    Cream Butter & Sugars

    In a large bowl, beat butter, brown sugar, and white sugar until light and fluffy.

  2. 2

    Add Wet Ingredients

    Add eggs one at a time, then mix in vanilla extract.

  3. 3

    Mix Dry Ingredients

    In another bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add to the wet mixture.

  4. 4

    Stir in Chocolate Chips

    Fold in the chocolate chips with a spatula until evenly distributed.

  5. 5

    Scoop and Bake

    Scoop tablespoon-sized dough balls onto a baking tray. Bake at 180¬∞C (350¬∞F) for 10‚Äì12 minutes until golden edges appear.

  6. 6

    Cool and Serve

    Let cookies cool on the tray for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack. Enjoy warm or store in an airtight container.

Chef's Tips

  • Use semi-sweet or dark chocolate chips for balanced sweetness.
  • Chill the dough for 30 mins before baking for thicker cookies.
  • Try adding chopped nuts for extra crunch.
  • Cookies can be stored for up to 5 days at room temperature.
