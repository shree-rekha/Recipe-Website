Frozen Yogurt Parfait

A refreshing layered dessert made with creamy yogurt, fresh fruits, and crunchy granola. Perfect for a light dessert or a healthy snack.

Prep: 10 mins
Freeze: 1 hr
Serves: 2
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plain or vanilla Greek yogurt
  • 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • 1/2 cup granola
  • 1/2 cup fresh berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • Mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. 1

    Prepare the Yogurt

    Mix the yogurt with honey or maple syrup if using. Stir until smooth and creamy.

  2. 2

    Layer the Parfait

    In a glass or jar, layer yogurt, granola, and fruits. Repeat the layers until the glass is full.

  3. 3

    Freeze and Serve

    Freeze for 1 hour for a chilled parfait or serve immediately if you prefer soft yogurt texture.

Chef's Tips

  • Use flavored yogurt for variety ‚Äì vanilla, strawberry, or mango.
  • Top with a drizzle of chocolate or fruit syrup before serving.
  • Add nuts or seeds for extra crunch and nutrition.
  • Great for breakfast, post-workout snack, or a light dessert.
