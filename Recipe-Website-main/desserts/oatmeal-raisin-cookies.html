Oatmeal Raisin Cookies

These chewy oatmeal raisin cookies are warm, comforting, and loaded with hearty oats and sweet raisins‚Äîperfect with a glass of milk!

Prep: 15 mins
Bake: 12 mins
Makes: 24 cookies
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (225g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup raisins

Instructions

  1. 1

    Cream the Butter & Sugars

    In a large bowl, beat together butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until smooth and creamy.

  2. 2

    Mix Wet Ingredients

    Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in vanilla extract.

  3. 3

    Combine Dry Ingredients

    In another bowl, mix flour, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt. Gradually add to the wet ingredients.

  4. 4

    Add Oats & Raisins

    Stir in the oats and raisins with a spoon or spatula until fully incorporated.

  5. 5

    Shape and Bake

    Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet. Bake at 180¬∞C (350¬∞F) for 10‚Äì12 minutes or until golden.

  6. 6

    Cool and Enjoy

    Cool on the tray for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

Chef's Tips

  • Soak raisins in warm water for 10 minutes to keep them plump.
  • Add chopped nuts or chocolate chips for variation.
  • Use old-fashioned rolled oats, not instant oats, for texture.
  • Store in an airtight container for up to a week.
