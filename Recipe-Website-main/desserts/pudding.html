Classic Vanilla Custard

Silky smooth custard flavored with vanilla bean.

  1. Heat milk, cream, and vanilla until warm.
  2. Whisk egg yolks with sugar, then slowly add hot milk.
  3. Cook on low heat until thickened.
  4. Chill before serving.

Chocolate Pudding

Rich and creamy homemade chocolate pudding.

  1. Combine cocoa, sugar, cornstarch, and milk.
  2. Cook over medium heat until thickened.
  3. Stir in butter and vanilla.
  4. Chill and serve with whipped cream.
