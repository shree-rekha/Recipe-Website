Rich Chocolate Cake

A deliciously rich and moist chocolate cake perfect for celebrations or indulgent desserts. This treat is sure to impress!

Prep: 20 mins
Cook: 35‚Äì40 mins
Serves: 8
Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup cooled black coffee
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. 1

    Prep the Oven

    Preheat oven to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C). Grease and dust two 9-inch round cake pans with cocoa powder.

  2. 2

    Mix Dry Ingredients

    In a large bowl, whisk together flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.

  3. 3

    Mix Wet Ingredients

    In a separate bowl, whisk eggs, then add buttermilk, coffee, oil, and vanilla extract. Combine well.

  4. 4

    Make the Batter

    Slowly add wet ingredients into the dry mixture and stir until just combined.

  5. 5

    Bake

    Pour batter evenly into pans. Bake for 30‚Äì35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.

  6. 6

    Cool & Frost

    Cool cakes in pans for 10 mins, then transfer to a rack. Let cool completely before frosting.
