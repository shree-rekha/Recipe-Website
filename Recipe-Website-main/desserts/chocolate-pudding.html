Chocolate Pudding

A rich and creamy chocolate dessert made from scratch with simple ingredients. Perfect served warm or chilled, topped with whipped cream or chocolate shavings.

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 10 mins
Serves: 4
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter

Instructions

  1. 1

    Mix Dry Ingredients

    In a saucepan, whisk together sugar, cocoa powder, cornstarch, and salt.

  2. 2

    Add Milk

    Gradually whisk in the milk until the mixture is smooth and lump-free.

  3. 3

    Cook the Mixture

    Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and begins to bubble (about 8‚Äì10 minutes).

  4. 4

    Add Butter and Vanilla

    Remove from heat and stir in butter and vanilla extract until fully incorporated.

  5. 5

    Cool and Serve

    Pour into serving bowls. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour or serve warm. Top with whipped cream if desired.

Chef's Tips

  • Whisk continuously to avoid lumps or scorching.
  • Use dark cocoa powder for a richer chocolate flavor.
  • Cover pudding with plastic wrap touching the surface to prevent a skin from forming.
  • Top with chocolate shavings or berries for extra indulgence.
