Vanilla Ice Cream

Classic creamy vanilla ice cream made from scratch with simple ingredients. Perfect for hot days or as a base for sundaes and floats.

Prep: 15 mins
Churn/Freeze: 4‚Äì6 hrs
Serves: 6
Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tbsp pure vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. 1

    Mix the Base

    In a large bowl, whisk together heavy cream, milk, sugar, vanilla, and salt until the sugar is completely dissolved.

  2. 2

    Chill the Mixture

    Cover the bowl and refrigerate the mixture for at least 2 hours or overnight for best flavor and texture.

  3. 3

    Churn

    Pour the chilled mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions (usually 20‚Äì25 minutes).

  4. 4

    Freeze

    Transfer churned ice cream to a container and freeze for 4‚Äì6 hours or until firm. Serve and enjoy!

Chef's Tips

  • No ice cream maker? Pour mixture into a container and stir every 30 mins while freezing for 3 hours.
  • Add mix-ins like chocolate chips or berries after churning.
  • Use real vanilla extract or vanilla bean for a richer flavor.
  • Store in an airtight container to prevent ice crystals.
