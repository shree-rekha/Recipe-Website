Vanilla Ice Cream

Creamy homemade vanilla ice cream with real vanilla bean.

  1. Whisk cream, milk, sugar, and vanilla together.
  2. Churn in an ice cream maker until thick.
  3. Freeze for 2-4 hours before serving.
  4. Serve with toppings or on its own.

Mango Sorbet

Refreshing tropical mango sorbet with just 3 ingredients.

  1. Blend fresh mango, sugar, and lemon juice.
  2. Churn or freeze in a container, stirring every hour.
  3. Freeze solid and scoop when ready.
  4. Garnish with mint if desired.

Frozen Yogurt Parfait

Layers of frozen yogurt, granola, and fruit.

  1. Spoon frozen yogurt into a glass.
  2. Add layers of granola, berries, and honey.
  3. Repeat layers and top with nuts or coconut.
  4. Serve immediately while still frozen.
