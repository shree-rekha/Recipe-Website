Red Velvet Cake

A classic moist and fluffy red velvet cake with a hint of cocoa, paired perfectly with cream cheese frosting. Ideal for birthdays and celebrations!

Prep: 25 mins
Cook: 30 mins
Serves: 8‚Äì10
Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 tsp white vinegar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp red food coloring
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs

Instructions

  1. 1

    Preheat and Prep

    Preheat oven to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.

  2. 2

    Mix Dry Ingredients

    In a bowl, sift together flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.

  3. 3

    Beat Butter and Sugar

    In a separate large bowl, cream butter and sugar until fluffy. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each.

  4. 4

    Add Wet Ingredients

    Mix in vanilla, buttermilk, vinegar, and red food coloring.

  5. 5

    Combine and Bake

    Gradually mix dry ingredients into wet until just combined. Pour batter evenly into pans and bake for 25‚Äì30 mins or until a toothpick comes out clean.

  6. 6

    Cool and Frost

    Let cakes cool completely before frosting with cream cheese frosting.

Chef's Tips

  • Use gel food coloring for a deeper red hue without affecting texture.
  • Ensure eggs and butter are at room temperature.
  • Chill the cake before slicing for cleaner cuts.
  • Pairs well with fresh berries or a dollop of whipped cream.
