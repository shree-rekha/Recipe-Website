Hummus and Pita

Creamy, smooth hummus paired with warm, soft pita bread‚Äîthis Mediterranean favorite is perfect for dipping, snacking, or starting a delicious lunch.

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 5 mins
Serves: 4
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained
  • 1/4 cup tahini
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Water, as needed for consistency
  • 4 pita breads
  • Extra olive oil and paprika for garnish

Instructions

  1. 1

    Blend the hummus

    Add chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, garlic, olive oil, and salt into a food processor. Blend until smooth.

  2. 2

    Adjust consistency

    Add a tablespoon of water at a time while blending until desired texture is achieved.

  3. 3

    Warm the pita

    Heat pita bread in a dry skillet or wrap in foil and warm in the oven for a few minutes.

  4. 4

    Serve

    Transfer hummus to a bowl, drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with paprika, and serve with warm pita slices.

Chef's Tips

  • Use ice-cold water while blending for extra creamy hummus.
  • Roast the garlic for a milder flavor.
  • Top with parsley or sumac for garnish and color.
  • Pairs well with fresh veggies like carrots and cucumber slices.
