Tomato Basil Soup

Rich and creamy tomato soup with fresh basil.

  1. Cook onions and garlic in olive oil.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes and simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. Blend smooth and stir in cream and basil.
  4. Serve hot with bread or croutons.

Sweet Corn Soup

Delicate Indo-Chinese style soup with sweet corn and veggies.

  1. Boil corn, carrots, and beans in water or broth.
  2. Add corn paste and cook until thickened.
  3. Season with salt, pepper, and a splash of soy sauce.
  4. Garnish with spring onions.
