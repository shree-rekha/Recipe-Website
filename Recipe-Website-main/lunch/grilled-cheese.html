Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Golden, crispy bread with gooey melted cheese‚Äîthis timeless sandwich is comfort food at its finest. Serve it with tomato soup or ketchup for the perfect lunch combo.

Prep: 5 mins
Cook: 5 mins
Serves: 1
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (white or whole wheat)
  • 2 slices of cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, or American)
  • 1 tbsp butter (softened)
  • Optional: tomato ketchup or soup for serving

Instructions

  1. 1

    Butter the Bread

    Spread butter evenly on one side of each bread slice.

  2. 2

    Add Cheese

    Place the cheese between the unbuttered sides of the bread to make a sandwich.

  3. 3

    Grill

    Heat a skillet over medium heat and grill the sandwich until golden brown on both sides and the cheese is melted.

  4. 4

    Serve

    Serve hot with tomato soup or ketchup.

Chef's Tips

  • Use a lid to trap heat and melt the cheese evenly.
  • Try adding tomato or caramelized onions for variation.
  • Use shredded cheese for faster melting.
  • Butter can be replaced with mayonnaise for a crispier finish.
