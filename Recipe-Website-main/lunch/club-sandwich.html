Club Sandwich

A stacked delight with layers of chicken, crispy vegetables, and creamy mayo between toasted bread. Perfect for lunch or a light dinner.

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 10 mins
Serves: 1‚Äì2
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 3 slices of bread
  • 1/2 cup cooked shredded chicken
  • 2 lettuce leaves
  • 2 slices tomato
  • 2 slices cucumber
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise
  • Butter for toasting
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. 1

    Toast the Bread

    Lightly butter and toast the bread slices until golden brown.

  2. 2

    Prepare Filling

    Mix the cooked chicken with mayonnaise, salt, and pepper.

  3. 3

    Layer the Sandwich

    Start with one slice of toast, add lettuce and chicken mix. Top with second slice, add tomato, cucumber, and more lettuce. Place the final slice of toast on top.

  4. 4

    Slice and Serve

    Secure with toothpicks and cut diagonally into quarters. Serve fresh.

Chef's Tips

  • Use rotisserie or leftover chicken for quicker prep.
  • Add a slice of cheese or crispy bacon for extra flavor.
  • Serve with fries or a side salad for a complete meal.
  • You can swap chicken with boiled eggs or tofu for variation.
