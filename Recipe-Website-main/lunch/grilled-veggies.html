Grilled Mediterranean Vegetables

A colorful medley of vegetables grilled to perfection with herbs and olive oil‚Äîperfect as a side dish, salad topper, or light lunch option.

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 15 mins
Serves: 4
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 eggplant, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano or Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: balsamic glaze or feta cheese for serving

Instructions

  1. 1

    Prep the veggies

    Slice all vegetables into even-sized pieces for uniform grilling.

  2. 2

    Season

    In a large bowl, toss the vegetables with olive oil, garlic powder, oregano, salt, and pepper.

  3. 3

    Grill

    Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Cook vegetables in batches until tender and lightly charred (3‚Äì5 minutes per side).

  4. 4

    Serve

    Arrange grilled veggies on a platter. Optional: drizzle with balsamic glaze or sprinkle with crumbled feta cheese.

Chef's Tips

  • Use a grill basket for smaller vegetables like cherry tomatoes.
  • Marinate veggies for 10 minutes before grilling for enhanced flavor.
  • Leftovers can be added to sandwiches, pasta, or wraps.
  • Don't overcrowd the grill‚Äîgrill in batches if needed.
