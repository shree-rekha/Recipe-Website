Mexican Salad Bowl

A vibrant and bold salad bowl loaded with rice, beans, salsa, guacamole, and fresh toppings. It‚Äôs a festive lunch full of flavor and color!

Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 10 mins
Serves: 2
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked white or brown rice
  • 1/2 cup kidney beans or black beans
  • 1/2 cup corn kernels
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/2 cup tomato salsa
  • 1/2 cup guacamole
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
  • Sliced jalape√±os (optional)
  • 1 lime wedge (for garnish)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. 1

    Prepare the Ingredients

    Cook rice and warm beans and corn. Wash and shred lettuce. Prepare salsa and guacamole or use store-bought versions.

  2. 2

    Layer the Bowl

    In a serving bowl, layer rice at the bottom. Add beans, corn, lettuce, salsa, and guacamole side-by-side.

  3. 3

    Add Toppings

    Sprinkle shredded cheese on top. Add jalape√±os if using. Garnish with a lime wedge for extra zest.

  4. 4

    Serve and Enjoy

    Serve immediately or chill slightly before serving. Mix all ingredients before eating for full flavor!

Chef's Tips

  • Use brown rice or quinoa for a healthier base.
  • Add grilled chicken or tofu for extra protein.
  • Homemade salsa and guacamole elevate the flavor.
  • Serve in a mason jar for a fun portable lunch!
