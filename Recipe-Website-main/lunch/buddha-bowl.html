Buddha Bowl

A colorful and balanced bowl loaded with grains, greens, roasted veggies, and protein, topped with a creamy tahini lemon dressing. Perfect for a healthy Mediterranean-style lunch.

Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 20 mins
Serves: 2
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked brown rice or quinoa
  • 1 cup spinach or mixed greens
  • 1/2 cup roasted sweet potatoes
  • 1/2 cup roasted chickpeas or tofu cubes
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 avocado, sliced
  • 2 tbsp tahini
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp maple syrup or honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: sesame seeds, pumpkin seeds

Instructions

  1. 1

    Prepare the Base

    Cook brown rice or quinoa as per package instructions and let cool slightly.

  2. 2

    Roast the Veggies

    Chop and roast sweet potatoes and chickpeas (or tofu) with olive oil, salt, and pepper at 200¬∞C (400¬∞F) for 20 minutes.

  3. 3

    Make Tahini Dressing

    Mix tahini, lemon juice, maple syrup, salt, and a little water until creamy and smooth.

  4. 4

    Assemble the Bowl

    Layer rice/quinoa, greens, roasted veggies, carrots, and avocado. Drizzle with tahini dressing and sprinkle seeds.

Chef's Tips

  • Use leftovers for toppings like grilled veggies or beans.
  • For crunch, add roasted nuts or seeds.
  • Chill the dressing before drizzling for a thicker texture.
  • Double the tahini dressing and store for later use.
