Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Golden, crispy bread with melted cheese in the center.

  1. Butter one side of each bread slice.
  2. Place cheese between unbuttered sides.
  3. Grill on a skillet until golden on both sides.
  4. Serve hot with tomato soup or ketchup.

Club Sandwich

Triple-layered sandwich with chicken, veggies, and mayo.

  1. Toast three bread slices.
  2. Layer with cooked chicken, lettuce, tomato, and mayonnaise.
  3. Stack and slice into quarters.
  4. Secure with toothpicks and serve.

Veggie Sandwich

A fresh sandwich packed with vegetables and hummus.

  1. Spread hummus or cream cheese on bread.
  2. Add lettuce, tomato, cucumber, bell pepper, and onion.
  3. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and herbs.
  4. Close and grill or serve cold.
