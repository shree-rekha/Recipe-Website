Quinoa Salad Bowl

Nutritious bowl with quinoa, black beans, corn, and avocado.

  1. Cook quinoa and let it cool.
  2. Mix with black beans, corn, bell peppers, and avocado.
  3. Drizzle with lime juice and olive oil.
  4. Top with fresh coriander and feta (optional).

Buddha Bowl

A balanced bowl with grains, greens, chickpeas, and tahini dressing.

  1. Layer brown rice or quinoa, spinach, and roasted veggies.
  2. Add chickpeas or tofu.
  3. Drizzle with tahini lemon dressing.
  4. Top with seeds or nuts for crunch.
