Quinoa Salad Bowl

A colorful, healthy lunch bowl featuring protein-rich quinoa, black beans, corn, and creamy avocado. Perfect for a nourishing midday meal.

Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 15 mins
Serves: 2
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup canned black beans (rinsed)
  • 1/2 cup corn (fresh or canned)
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 avocado, cubed
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (optional)
  • Crumbled feta cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. 1

    Cook and cool the quinoa

    Rinse and cook quinoa according to package instructions. Allow to cool completely.

  2. 2

    Mix the ingredients

    In a large bowl, combine quinoa, black beans, corn, bell pepper, and avocado.

  3. 3

    Dress the salad

    Drizzle with olive oil and lime juice. Add salt and pepper. Toss gently.

  4. 4

    Garnish and serve

    Top with fresh coriander and crumbled feta, if desired. Serve chilled or at room temperature.

Chef's Tips

  • Use tri-color quinoa for extra visual appeal.
  • Chill the quinoa before mixing to prevent avocado from becoming mushy.
  • Add a diced jalape√±o for a spicy kick.
  • Make ahead and store in the fridge for up to 2 days.
