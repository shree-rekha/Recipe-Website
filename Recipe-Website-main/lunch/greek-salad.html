Greek Salad

A fresh, colorful, and healthy Mediterranean salad loaded with tomatoes, cucumbers, onions, olives, and creamy feta. Perfect as a light lunch or a side dish.

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 0 mins
Serves: 4
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chopped tomatoes
  • 1 cup sliced cucumbers
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup kalamata olives
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. 1

    Prep vegetables

    Wash and chop the tomatoes, cucumbers, and onions. Add to a large salad bowl.

  2. 2

    Combine ingredients

    Add olives and crumbled feta cheese to the bowl with the vegetables.

  3. 3

    Make the dressing

    In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper.

  4. 4

    Toss and serve

    Pour dressing over the salad and toss gently. Serve chilled or at room temperature.

Chef's Tips

  • Use ripe, juicy tomatoes for best flavor.
  • Try adding fresh mint or basil for a twist.
  • Let the salad sit for 10 minutes before serving to let flavors meld.
  • Pairs wonderfully with grilled chicken or pita bread.
