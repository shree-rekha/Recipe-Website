Chicken Noodle Soup

Warm, nourishing, and packed with flavor, this classic chicken noodle soup is the ultimate comfort food perfect for any lunch.

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 30 mins
Serves: 4
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup cooked, shredded chicken
  • 1.5 cups egg noodles
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. 1

    Saut√© vegetables

    In a large pot, melt butter over medium heat. Add chopped onions, carrots, and celery. Cook until slightly tender.

  2. 2

    Add broth and chicken

    Pour in the chicken broth. Stir in shredded chicken and thyme. Bring to a gentle boil.

  3. 3

    Cook noodles

    Add egg noodles and cook according to package directions (usually about 8‚Äì10 minutes), or until tender.

  4. 4

    Season and serve

    Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot and garnish with fresh parsley if desired.

Chef's Tips

  • Use rotisserie chicken for convenience and rich flavor.
  • Try adding fresh herbs like dill or parsley before serving.
  • Substitute egg noodles with pasta or rice for variation.
  • Freeze leftovers for a quick meal later.
