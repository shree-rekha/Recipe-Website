Mediterranean Lunch Recipes

Greek Salad

A refreshing salad with tomatoes, cucumbers, olives, and feta cheese.

  1. Chop tomatoes, cucumbers, and onions.
  2. Add olives and crumbled feta cheese.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and vinegar, season with oregano.
  4. Toss and serve chilled.

Hummus and Pita

Classic creamy hummus served with warm pita bread.

  1. Blend chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, garlic, and olive oil.
  2. Adjust consistency and seasoning as needed.
  3. Warm pita bread on a skillet or in the oven.
  4. Serve hummus with pita slices and olive oil drizzle.

Grilled Mediterranean Vegetables

Seasoned vegetables grilled with olive oil and herbs.

  1. Slice zucchini, eggplant, peppers, and onions.
  2. Toss in olive oil, garlic, and mixed herbs.
  3. Grill until charred and tender.
  4. Serve with balsamic drizzle or feta on top.
