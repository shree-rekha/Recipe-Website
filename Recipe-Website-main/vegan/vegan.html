Vegan Chickpea Wrap

A protein-rich wrap made with mashed chickpeas and fresh veggies.

  1. Mash chickpeas with vegan mayo, lemon juice, and seasonings.
  2. Add diced cucumber, bell pepper, and onion.
  3. Spread on a whole wheat tortilla and roll it up.
  4. Serve chilled or slightly warmed.

Iced Matcha Latte

A cool, dairy-free drink with almond milk and matcha powder.

  1. Whisk matcha powder with hot water until smooth.
  2. Fill a glass with ice and pour over almond or oat milk.
  3. Add matcha mixture and sweetener (optional).
  4. Stir and enjoy cold.
