Vegan Chickpea Wrap

A wholesome and filling wrap made with mashed chickpeas, fresh vegetables, and zesty vegan mayo. Perfect for a quick and healthy lunch!

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 0 mins
Serves: 2
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 2 tbsp vegan mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 small carrot, grated
  • 1 small cucumber, chopped
  • 1/4 red onion, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 large whole wheat or spinach wraps
  • A handful of lettuce or spinach leaves

Instructions

  1. 1

    Mash the chickpeas

    In a bowl, mash the chickpeas with a fork or potato masher until mostly broken down, leaving some texture.

  2. 2

    Mix the filling

    Add vegan mayo, mustard, grated carrot, cucumber, and red onion. Mix well. Season with salt and pepper.

  3. 3

    Assemble the wrap

    Lay the wrap flat. Add a few lettuce leaves, then spoon the chickpea mixture on top. Spread evenly.

  4. 4

    Wrap and serve

    Roll up the wrap tightly. Cut in half and serve immediately, or wrap in foil for a packed lunch.
