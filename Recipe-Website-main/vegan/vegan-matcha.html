Iced Matcha Latte

A refreshing and creamy Japanese green tea drink made with matcha, milk, and a touch of sweetness ‚Äî perfect for cooling down with a healthy twist.

Prep: 5 mins
No Cook
Serves: 1
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp matcha green tea powder
  • 2 tbsp hot water (not boiling)
  • 1‚Äì2 tsp maple syrup or sweetener of choice
  • 3/4 cup chilled oat milk or other plant milk
  • Ice cubes

Instructions

  1. 1

    Whisk the matcha

    In a small bowl, whisk matcha powder with hot water until smooth and frothy using a bamboo whisk or milk frother.

  2. 2

    Sweeten

    Add maple syrup or your preferred sweetener to the matcha mixture and stir to combine.

  3. 3

    Assemble the latte

    Fill a glass with ice, pour in chilled oat milk, then slowly pour the matcha mixture on top for a layered look.

  4. 4

    Stir & serve

    Stir gently to combine before drinking. Enjoy immediately!

Chef's Tips

  • Sift the matcha powder first to prevent clumps.
  • Use ceremonial grade matcha for best flavor and vibrant color.
  • Add vanilla extract for a flavor boost.
  • Swap oat milk with almond, soy, or coconut milk as desired.
