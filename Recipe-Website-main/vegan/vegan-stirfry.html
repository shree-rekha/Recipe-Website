Quick Vegan Stir-Fry

A vibrant and flavorful stir-fry loaded with fresh vegetables and a savory soy-garlic sauce. Ready in just 15 minutes‚Äîperfect for busy weeknights!

Prep: 5 mins
Cook: 10 mins
Serves: 2
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp sesame oil (or any vegetable oil)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 inch ginger, grated
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers, sliced
  • 1/2 cup broccoli florets
  • 1/4 cup carrots, julienned
  • 1/4 cup snap peas
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce (or tamari for gluten-free)
  • 1 tsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp maple syrup or agave
  • Optional: sesame seeds and green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. 1

    Heat the oil

    In a large skillet or wok, heat sesame oil over medium-high heat. Add garlic and ginger and saut√© for 30 seconds until fragrant.

  2. 2

    Stir-fry the veggies

    Add bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, and snap peas. Stir-fry for 4‚Äì5 minutes until vegetables are tender but still crisp.

  3. 3

    Add the sauce

    In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, rice vinegar, and maple syrup. Pour over the veggies and toss to coat evenly.

  4. 4

    Garnish and serve

    Remove from heat. Garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions. Serve hot with rice or noodles.

Chef's Tips

  • Use any seasonal vegetables you have on hand.
  • Add tofu or tempeh for extra protein.
  • Double the sauce if serving over rice or noodles.
  • Use chili flakes or sriracha for a spicy kick.
